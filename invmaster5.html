Where Can I Buy A Flytrap?

Consider if you can properly care for a Venus flytrap. These plants require a regular care regimen.

  1. You will be able to keep your Venus flytraps inside or outside, planted in pots.
  2. Venus flytraps will need a good deal of sunlight and warmth for most of their growing cycle.
  3. You will need to have patience, as Venus flytraps require a period of dormancy during the winter. They will grow less and require grooming.
  4. Be prepared to trim dead leaves and blooms off of the plant to keep it healthy and to water the plant on a schedule.
  5. You may need to feed the trap with crickets or other insects if it doesn't catch many on its own.

I don't really have that much stuff...

You might be surprised! If you had to itemize all of your personal possessions, what dollar amount would you place on them? Most people grossly underestimate the value of the contents of their residence, and end up not getting properly compensated for those losses when they occur.

My bedroom is worth HOW MUCH?!!

Would you believe that just your bedroom could have over $5,000 in personal possessions hiding in it? When you start adding up each article of clothing, footwear, outdoor wear, bedding, small furniture, electronics and decorations, the total cost of these items starts accumulating dramatically. Without a detailed list of these items, you could be seriously undervaluing the total worth of this small portion of your belongings and short-change yourself should you need to replace them!
